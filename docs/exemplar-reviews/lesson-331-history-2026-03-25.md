## Summary

Lesson `331` should not be approved as the history/social studies exemplar.

The rejection is driven by two separate problems:
- canonical choice is unresolved because the module also contains public sibling lesson `3283` with the same title
- the lesson describes source-analysis activities, but it does not actually anchor the student in a concrete source, excerpt, or piece of evidence strongly enough to serve as the guide pattern

This is not a “bad lesson” ruling.

It is a “not safe to copy as the model” ruling.
