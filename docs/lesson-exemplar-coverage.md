## Approval Notes

Approved exemplar means:
- the lesson is the one the team wants others to copy for that archetype
- the lesson passes the gold rubric and review checklist for that archetype
- the lesson is stable enough to guide rewrites, audits, and future exemplar comparisons

Not yet approved means:
- the archetype spec exists, but there is no canonical guide lesson yet
- the archetype can still be used for planning, but not as a rewrite exemplar

The current repo reality is:
- `1437` is the approved elementary-math guide exemplar
- the other archetypes have specs, but no approved exemplar lessons yet
